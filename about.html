Mission Statement

Our mission is simple: Make dependable gear accessible to everyone. We aim to support our community with fair prices, honest descriptions, and gear that works as hard as you do. In the field, on the trail, or in daily life.

Why Choose Us?

  • Authentic Surplus & Modern Gear - No knockoffs, no compromises.
  • Fair Pricing - We believe good gear shouldn't cost a fortune.
  • Community Focused - Helping friends, veterans, and outdoor enthusiasts.
  • Global Mindset - PH-based, US-connected.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell replicas or authentic surplus?

All items listed are authentic surplus unless otherwise stated. We also clearly note reproduction gear for transparency.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we provide international shipping with tracking via postal and courier services.

Are your products new or used?

Most items are issued surplus, meaning they may show light wear. We always include condition descriptions in listings.

Can I request a specific item?

Yes! If you’re looking for something not listed, contact us — we’ll try to source it for you.
